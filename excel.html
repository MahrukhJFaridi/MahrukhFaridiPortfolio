Bike Sales in Europe based on Demographics

This MS Excel project delved into analyzing bike sales in Europe. Beginning with data cleanup using Excel functions and formulas, I ensured accuracy and consistency. Next, I constructed three pivot tables, illuminating sales trends based on average income levels for men and women, commute distance, and age brackets. Surprisingly, findings indicated that women with higher salaries and men with lower salaries were inclined to purchase bikes. Additionally, the data highlighted a notable preference among middle-aged individuals for bike purchases over the youth demographic. Building upon these insights, I developed a comprehensive dashboard showcasing intricate bike sales patterns. This holistic approach provided with actionable insights to navigate the dynamic European bike market landscape effectively.
