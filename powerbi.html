Wellness and Life-style Survey Analysis

This Power BI project offers a comprehensive breakdown of a wellness and lifestyle survey conducted with an impressive participation of almost 16,000 individuals. Through meticulous data analysis, it unveils intriguing insights into various aspects of respondents' lives.

One notable finding indicates a gender disparity in work-life balance, with women showcasing a higher equilibrium between professional and personal spheres compared to men. Additionally, the survey reveals that men grapple with daily stress more prominently than women, with a staggering 75% of men reporting stress compared to a mere 25% of women.

Age emerges as a significant factor influencing behaviors and attitudes. The data suggests that older individuals exhibit a greater propensity for charitable giving, underscoring a generational trend in philanthropy. The youth demonstrate a heightened engagement in social networking platforms, while the elderly are more inclined towards leisure pursuits such as hobbies.

Moreover, a remarkable 80% of survey participants express contentment with their current salary levels, shedding light on the prevailing sentiments regarding financial satisfaction among the sampled population.
